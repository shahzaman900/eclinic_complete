Why Choose Us

eClinic is a healthcare project initiated by Cognitive Healthcare International, that focuses on providing Preventative Care using advanced technology & customizable treatment plans for your well-being and betterment.

At eClinic, we offer quick & easy diagnosis and results using smart medical devices, a pool of certified medical professionals available for consultation & a cloud-based infrastructure, keeping you & the clinic connected at all times.

We provide quality care for acute, palliative and chronic cases while also ensuring the patient’s feasibility & condition, through services like remote patient monitoring, assisted homecare & in-app teleconsultation.
